Pop Music
Pop is the world's most accessible and commercially successful genre, designed for broad appeal and catchy hooks. Key characteristics:
- Upbeat melodies and singalong choruses
- Polished, radio-friendly production
- Often uplifting emotional tones (happy, empowering)
- Varied energy levels from mid-tempo grooves to high-energy dance anthems
- Modern instrumentation blending electronic and acoustic elements
- Typically 3-4 minute songs optimized for streaming and radio play

Pop thrives on emotion, relatability, and memorability. Whether celebrating joy, heartbreak, or confidence, pop melodies embed themselves in your mind.
Subgenres include indie pop (experimental), electropop (synth-heavy), and pop-rock (guitar-driven).
