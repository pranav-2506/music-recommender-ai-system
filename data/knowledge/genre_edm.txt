Electronic Dance Music (EDM)
EDM is produced entirely using synthesizers, drum machines, and digital production tools. Signature features:
- Pulsing, repetitive beats (often 120-140+ BPM)
- Layered synthesizer melodies and basslines
- Build-ups and drops for energy peaks
- Minimal vocal presence (often sampled or absent)
- High danceability and rhythmic precision
- Euphoric, energetic emotional content

EDM is designed for movement, celebration, and club/festival environments. It maximizes energy and group connection.
Subgenres include house (steady groove), dubstep (aggressive bass), trance (hypnotic), and drum and bass (complex patterns).
EDM production emphasizes sonic texture and rhythmic innovation over traditional melody and harmony.
