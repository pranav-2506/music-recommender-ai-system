Chill Mood
A chill mood encompasses relaxation, ease, and a low-stress state of mind. Music for chilling should:
- Have slower tempos (60-90 BPM range)
- Feature smooth, gentle instrumentation
- Avoid sudden loud spikes or aggressive sounds
- Create a sense of comfort and safety
- Often incorporate ambient or acoustic elements
- Promote peace without stimulating energy or excitement

Chill music is ideal for unwinding after a long day, studying, working, hanging out with friends in a calm setting, or just existing without pressure.
It's the soundtrack to comfort: cozy moments, quiet evenings, recovery, and mental rest.
Genres that fit: lofi, ambient, jazz, some indie, some electronic. The key is the vibe, not the genre.
