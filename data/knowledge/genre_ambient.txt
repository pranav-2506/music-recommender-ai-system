Ambient Music
Ambient music creates immersive sound environments designed for passive listening and atmosphere. Key characteristics:
- Slow, evolving soundscapes with minimal melody
- Often lacks traditional song structure (no verse-chorus pattern)
- Focuses on texture, timbre, and space rather than rhythm
- Very low energy and minimal percussion
- Encourages introspection and relaxation
- Often influenced by electronic production and field recordings

Ambient is perfect for meditation, background focus, sleep, and creating mood. It functions as an enveloping environment rather than foreground entertainment.
Pioneers like Brian Eno defined ambient as music that "asks for no attention" and instead "rewards attention" when given.
Subgenres include drone (single sustained tones), ambient electronic, and ambient classical.
