Lofi (Low Fidelity) Music
Lofi hip-hop and lofi beats are characterized by a relaxed, atmospheric production style. These tracks often feature:
- Jazzy chord progressions and smooth samples
- Laid-back, minimal percussion
- Intentionally imperfect production for a cozy, "in-the-moment" feel
- Samples from older records, vinyl crackle, tape saturation
- Typically slower tempos (70-90 BPM)
- High acousticness reflecting its jazz and analog origins

Lofi is perfect for studying, working, sleeping, and creating. It creates a calm, focused environment without demanding attention.
The genre emerged from hip-hop production and internet communities sharing lo-fi beats online, becoming a staple of gaming, studying, and relaxation streams.
