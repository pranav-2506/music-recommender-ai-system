Rock Music
Rock is built on guitar-driven melodies, powerful rhythm sections, and emotional intensity. Core elements:
- Electric guitar as the primary voice (lead, rhythm, solos)
- Strong, driving drum patterns
- Bold, assertive vocals
- Energy ranges from bluesy slow burns to explosive crescendos
- Often raw, less polished production that emphasizes authenticity
- Themes: rebellion, freedom, love, struggle, empowerment

Rock creates a visceral, energetic listening experience. It's ideal for motivation, exercise, and passionate moments.
Subgenres span from hard rock and metal (extreme intensity) to indie rock (experimental, lo-fi) to classic rock (timeless anthems).
Rock has been a voice of cultural change and personal expression since the 1950s.
